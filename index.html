The Opportunity

Why Partner With Us?

Partnering with Heartfulness Bengaluru connects your brand with a movement backed by 80+ years of institutional credibility, UN affiliation, and NITI Aayog registration - reaching a values-driven community of over 10,000 practitioners across the city.

01 - CSR & ESG Strong CSR and ESG Alignment

Our initiatives span environment, wellness, and education - qualifying under Schedule VII CSR categories. Heartfulness is NITI Aayog registered with 80G/12A tax exemption, making every contribution directly deductible.

02 - Brand Visibility High-Impact Brand Presence

Your brand appears across jerseys, stumps, boundary boards, stage backdrops, certificates, merchandise, and all digital creatives - with dedicated social media campaigns on Instagram, YouTube, and emailers reaching Heartfulness Bengaluru's community.

03 - Engaged Audience Access to a Values-Driven Community

Reach professionals, families, and youth from across Bengaluru - a demographic that is health-conscious, community-oriented, and highly engaged. This is not a passive audience; these are people who show up.

04 - Employee Engagement Activate Your Own Team

Invite your employees to participate, volunteer, or attend - creating a meaningful touchpoint around wellness, sportsmanship, and community purpose. A genuinely differentiated team engagement opportunity.

05 - Global Credibility Backed by a World-Class Institution

Heartfulness is a UN-affiliated global movement in 160 countries, a Guinness World Record holder, and home to the world's largest meditation centre at Kanha Shanti Vanam. Your association carries institutional weight.

06 - Zero Overhead 100% Impact, Fully Documented

Every rupee contributed goes directly to programme delivery - all events are volunteer-run with zero administrative overhead. Full impact documentation provided for your internal CSR reporting.

Global Movement

About Heartfulness

Heartfulness Institute is a global non-profit dedicated to heart-based meditation, guided by Daaji, Kamlesh D. Patel - the fourth Guide of the Heartfulness lineage. From a handful of seekers to millions of practitioners across 160 countries, Heartfulness is one of the world's fastest-growing wellness movements.

Our Chapter

Heartfulness Bengaluru

One of the most vibrant chapters of the global Heartfulness movement, serving Bengaluru's seekers, professionals, and families for over 50 years.

In a city defined by relentless pace, Heartfulness Bengaluru offers a rare sanctuary - a space to pause, breathe, and reconnect. With over 10,000 practitioners across five key centres, our community continues to grow.

Our Initiatives

Forests by Heartfulness

Regular plantation drives across Bengaluru, including the flagship Green Heartfulness Run at Cubbon Park - thousands of trees planted annually through community-led reforestation efforts in partnership with local authorities.

Heartfulness Sports League

A dedicated multi-sport platform anchored by the Heartfulness Premier League (HPL) cricket tournament - building community and camaraderie across all age groups, with 100% of proceeds going to the Heartfulness Institute.

Youth Engagement

The Bengaluru Youth Seminar alongside a year-round calendar of city-wide events and collaborations with the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports - nurturing the next generation of conscious leaders.

University & Corporate Connect

Tailored Heartfulness wellness programmes delivered directly into Bengaluru's leading universities and corporate campuses - helping organisations build genuine cultures of wellbeing from the inside out.

Outstation Immersion Programme

Two-day residential retreats where Bengaluru youth travel together to Heartfulness centres in neighbouring towns - deepening their practice, forging real community bonds, and stepping away from the pace of city life.
